# Spec Engineering: The Mastery Curriculum

Welcome to your journey into **Spec Engineering**. This curriculum is designed
to transform you from a developer who writes code to an **Architect and
Orchestrator** who engineers intent.

## What is Spec Engineering?

Spec Engineering is the formal practice of defining human intent with enough
precision, structure, and verifiability that it can be autonomously implemented
by AI agents. It shifts the center of gravity in software development from the
**Implementation** (code) to the **Specification** (intent).

## Why This Matters

In the era of Large Language Models (LLMs), the primary bottleneck is no longer
"how to code" but **"how to specify."** Vague prompts lead to "vibe-coding,"
which results in architectural drift, regressions, and hallucinations. Spec
Engineering is the rigorous antidote.

- **Key Concepts:** Evals (Acceptance Criteria), Edge Case Analysis, Task
  Decomposition, Validation Loops.

## How to Master This

- **Treat Specs as Source Code:** Version them, review them, and keep them in
  your repository.
- **Iterate on Intent:** If the AI fails to implement a feature, don't fix the
  code—fix the spec.
- **Verify Rigorously:** A specification without automated validation is just a
  suggestion.
