# Intent Engineering

Intent Engineering is the strategic discipline of defining **outcomes, success
criteria, and constraints** to guide AI agents toward autonomous problem-solving.
While Prompt Engineering focuses on the tactical "how" of a single interaction,
Intent Engineering focuses on the "what" and "why" to achieve a business or
technical goal over a sustained workflow.

## Learning Path

This series of documents provides a comprehensive framework for mastering intent,

1. **[01-macro-strategy.md](01-macro-strategy.md)**: Outcome-Oriented Architecture.
   Focuses on defining the "What" and "Why," establishing success criteria, and

2. **[02-meso-workflow.md](02-meso-workflow.md)**: Specification & Reasoning.
   Focuses on the translation of vague human desires into structured

3. **[03-micro-implementation.md](03-micro-implementation.md)**: Precision &

## Core Philosophy

- **Outcome over Instruction**: Tell the agent *what* success looks like, not
  *how* to get there.
- **Guardrails over Micromanagement**: Define what the agent *must not* do,
  leaving the solution space open for creative problem-solving.
- **Verification is Finality**: An intent is only fulfilled when it passes a
  predetermined, measurable rubric of success.
- **Context is the Map, Intent is the Destination**: Provide the necessary
  background, but keep the focus on the target state.
