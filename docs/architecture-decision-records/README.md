# Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)

Welcome to the ADR learning series. This series will teach you how to document the "why" behind your technical choices, moving beyond tribal knowledge to a structured, versioned, and socialized decision-making process.

## Learning Path

- **[ADR Macro: The Decision Manifesto](./01-macro-strategy.md)**  
  Understand the philosophy of ADRs, why we use them, and how they solve the problem of "architectural drift" and tribal knowledge.
- **[ADR Meso: The Decision Workflow](./02-meso-workflow.md)**  
  Learn the tactical process of proposing, reviewing, and updating ADRs within a development lifecycle.
- **[ADR Micro: Implementation Standards](./03-micro-implementation.md)**  
  Get the concrete templates, naming conventions, and tooling recommendations for writing high-quality ADRs.

## Quick Start: Why ADRs?

In high-velocity teams, decisions are often made in Slack, Zoom, or over lunch. Without a record, these decisions become "shadow architecture"—hidden rules that everyone follows but nobody can explain. ADRs bring these decisions into the light, treating them as first-class artifacts in your repository.
