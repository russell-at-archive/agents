# CDD Mastery: From Macro to Micro

Welcome to the Contract-Driven Design (CDD) Mastery guide. This series is
designed to take you from a high-level strategic understanding of contract-first
development down to the granular details of implementation and verification.

## The Three Levels of CDD

CDD is most effective when applied across three distinct levels of abstraction:

1. [Macro Manifesto](./01-macro-manifesto.md): The "Why"
   - Focus: Strategic alignment, API-first principles, and decoupling.
   - Goal: Ensuring distributed systems can evolve independently.

2. [Meso Workflow](./02-meso-workflow.md): The "What"
   - Focus: Collaborative design, OpenAPI, and Consumer-Driven Contracts (CDC).
   - Goal: Defining formal agreements through shared schemas.

3. [Micro Implementation](./03-micro-implementation.md): The "How"
   - Focus: Tooling (Pact, Prism), contract testing in CI/CD, and validation.
   - Goal: Building and verifying the implementation against the contract.
