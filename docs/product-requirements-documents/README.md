Welcome to the PRD learning series. This series will teach you how to master the art of writing high-precision, AI-ready Product Requirements Documents that bridge the gap between business intent and technical implementation.

## Quick Start: Why PRDs?

In modern software development, the "how" (code) is increasingly handled by AI. The competitive advantage of a Product Manager or Senior Engineer lies in the **"why" and the "what"**. A PRD is your tool for ensuring that every line of code written—by human or machine—is in service of a validated user outcome.
