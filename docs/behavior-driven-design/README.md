# BDD Mastery: From Macro to Micro

Welcome to the Behavior-Driven Design (BDD) Mastery guide. This series is designed to take you from a high-level strategic understanding of BDD down to the granular details of implementation.

## The Three Levels of BDD

BDD is most effective when applied across three distinct levels of abstraction:

1.  **[Macro Strategy](./01-macro-strategy.md): The "Why"**
    *   Focus: Business outcomes, discovery, and ubiquitous language.
    *   Goal: Aligning stakeholders and ensuring we build the right thing.

2.  **[Meso Workflow](./02-meso-workflow.md): The "What"**
    *   Focus: Formulation, feature files, and executable specifications.
    *   Goal: Defining system behavior through collaborative scenarios.

3.  **[Micro Implementation](./03-micro-implementation.md): The "How"**
    *   Focus: Automation, step definitions, and technical verification.
    *   Goal: Building the thing right using a BDD-TDD inner/outer loop.
